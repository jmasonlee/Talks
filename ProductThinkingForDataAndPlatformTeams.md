# Product Thinking For Data And Platform Teams

## Talk Resources

While the talk is based on my personal experiences, these resources helped me better understand and explain the concepts:

### Product Thinking in General
- [The Platform As A Product Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS)
- [Shifting Mindsets: Why you should treat data as a product](https://www.thoughtworks.com/en-ca/insights/e-books/modern-data-engineering-playbook/data-as-a-product)

### Find a Win for your customer & team
   - If you only have 4 minutes, [start here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mg__aZ0S7kU&list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS&index=5) (Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais)
   - [Who are our customers, anyways?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iOc6bpYAQIA&list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS&index=6) (Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais)
   - Measuring the ROI of an investment in internal tooling: [Speak So They Can Hear You](https://jessitron.com/2025/11/16/speak-so-they-can-hear-you/) (Jessica Kerr)
### Iterate Early & Often
   - [I test in Production, and so should you](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b2oota_FhGY) (Charity Majors)
### Love the Problem, Not Your Solution
   - If you only have 4 minutes, [start here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S6eJKPi20jc&list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS&index=7) (Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais)
   - If you have another 4 minutes, [continue here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IGoUnPua-_U&list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS&index=8) (Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais)
### Think in Outcomes Over Outputs
   - If you only have 4 minutes, [start here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XRHm-CwzZ34&list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS&index=11) (Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais)
   - An interesting take on measuring the reduction in cognitive load that could be applied to platform or data products: [The TOIL Scorecard](https://jay.bazuzi.com/Toil-Scorecard/) (Jay Bazuzi)
### Make Your Code Easy To Change
   - Dashboards should be flexible tools for debugging: [Dashboards, or Launchpads?](https://thenewstack.io/dashboards-or-launchpads/) (Martin Thwaites)
   - A really great resource on how to structure observability data: [Data Strategy for SREs and Observability Teams](https://www.honeycomb.io/blog/data-strategy-sre-observability-teams) (Irving Popovetsky)
### Think in Datapoints and Discoveries Not Failures
 - If you only have 4 minutes, [start here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rSpfzLVJ27o&list=PLYTh9n7QJnzSnnCODHciMG4nTFHXcWNQS&index=10) (Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais)
### Data & Platform Engineers Influence Organizational Culture
 - [Platform Engineering: It's About Culture, not Tools (Bryan Ross)](https://about.gitlab.com/the-source/platform/platform-engineering-its-about-culture-not-tools/)
 - [My Model of Lived Culture (I wrote this one!)](https://jmasonlee.github.io/culture/engineering/leadership/2025/11/11/model_for_lived_culture.html)
